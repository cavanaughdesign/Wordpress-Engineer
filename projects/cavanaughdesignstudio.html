Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The new charges will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

How long does implementation take?

Basic implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks. Custom enterprise solutions may take 4-12 weeks depending on complexity and integration requirements.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise solutions include a custom demo and pilot program.

What kind of support is included?

All plans include email support. Professional plans include priority email and chat support. Enterprise plans include 24/7 dedicated support with a named account manager.

About Cavanaugh Design Studio

Our Story

Cavanaugh Design Studio was founded in 2018 by a team of AI researchers and software engineers with a shared vision: making artificial intelligence accessible and valuable for businesses of all sizes.

What began as a small startup has grown into a leading AI development company with over 100 employees across three continents, serving clients from innovative startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Our core belief is that AI should be designed with humans at the center – enhancing capabilities and creating new possibilities without replacing the human touch that makes businesses unique.

Our Values

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible with AI technology, investing heavily in research and development.

Human-Centered

We believe AI should augment human capabilities, not replace them. Our solutions are designed to enhance what makes your business special.

Ethical AI

We develop AI with strong ethical principles, ensuring fairness, transparency, privacy, and security in all our solutions.
